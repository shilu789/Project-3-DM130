About Us

Wild Rescues is a registered nonprofit wildlife rescue and rehabilitation facility located in Ocala, Florida. We help injured, sick, neglected, and orphaned wildlife. Our mission is to rehabilitate animals and release them back into the wild whenever possible.

We are proud members of the National Wildlife Rehabilitators Association

Watch the video below to meet our new, rescued friends.

We help many animals, such as:

  • Raccoons
  • Squirrels
  • Foxes
  • Birds
  • Horses
  • Deer
  • Pigs
  • Reptiles

Did you find an injured or orphaned animal? Contact us to see if we can help.
